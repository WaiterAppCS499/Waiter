Waiter

Restaurant Wait Times

View on GitHub

Go. Eat. Enjoy. Repeat.

Instead of calling restaurants in your area for wait times, enter your parties' information and get results instantly. Narrow down your options quickly and make eating out less of a hassle. Most importantly: Don't keep your friends waiting.

How it works

When users (that's you!) author a wait time post, its pushed to our database. From there other users can look up those wait times depending on their parties' information to decide which option is best for them.
